About Kofi Bonsu

Kofi Bonsu is an Afro-Soul artist, producer, and songwriter whose sound bridges the gap between traditional African rhythms and contemporary soul. Formerly known as Brizzy On Da Beat, he's spent years crafting a sonic identity that honors heritage while pushing forward.

His work has resonated with audiences across continents, blending live instrumentation with electronic production to create something unmistakably his own.

Story

Bio

Kofi Bonsu didn't arrive overnight. Born from years of grinding as Brizzy On Da Beat, he shed the alias to step into something truer — a sound that fuses the raw pulse of Afrobeat with the emotional depth of soul music. The name change wasn't rebranding. It was rebirth.

As a producer first, Kofi built his foundation in the studio, crafting beats for many artists before finding the courage to center his own voice. That journey — from behind the boards to the front of the stage — informs every note he plays now.

"The beat is the body. The melody is the spirit. I make music for both."

His latest single, "10," marks a milestone — a decade in the game, distilled into three minutes of pure intention. It's Afro-Soul in its truest form: rhythmic, melodic, and unapologetically human.

Whether he's performing live, producing in the studio, or building community through his art, Kofi Bonsu moves with one purpose — to create music that moves people.
